    My name is Álvaro Ribas, I am a postdoctoral researcher in the Department of Astronomy at Boston University. My research focuses on protoplanetary disks: disks of dust and gas around young stars in which planetary systems form. By using large samples of these disks and looking at their emission at different wavelengths and ages, I try to characterize their evolution and study how it impacts different aspects of planet formation.
