    Home

    I am a Research Associate at the Institute of Astronomy of the University of Cambridge. My research focuses on protoplanetary disks: disks of gas and dust around young stars, in which planetary systems are born. By using large samples of these disks and looking at their emission at different wavelengths, I aim at characterizing their evolution and study how it impacts different aspects of planet formation.
