    Observational experience & observing runs

    I have conducted several observing runs (photometry and spectroscopy) with the Isaac Newton Telescope at the Roque de los Muchachos Observatory (Canary Islands), within the context of the DANCe project to study the dynamics of nearby clusters of stars.

    I am the Principal Investigator of two high-priority accepted proposals to observe transitional disks in the mid-infrared with the Canaricam instrument at the Gran Telescopio Canarias.

    Also, I was co-Investigator of two accepted proposal with the Herschel Space Telescope.
